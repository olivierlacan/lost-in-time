1950's

4.11 - Cabin Fever

1960's

Θ 1960 - Emily Linus goes into labor prematurely while hiking 32 miles outside of Portland, Oregon with her husband, Roger Linus. She was 7 months pregnant. Emily dies after having given birth to Benjamin Linus.
3.20 - The Man Behind The Curtain
Θ 1961 - John Locke, age 5, is visited by Richard Alpert at Locke's foster home. Richard claims to be from a school for special kids, and gives Locke a test asking him to say which of four items (a compass, a knife, a comic book, a baseball mitt and a tube containing sand) "belong to him, already".
4.11 - Cabin Fever

1970's

Θ 1970 - The DHARMA Initiative is created.
2.03 - Orientation
Θ 1972 - Ben sees his mother Emily Linus for a second time and tries to run toward her. She calls to him to stop, as she does not want him to cross the sonar fence. She tells him it's not yet time for him to come.
3.20 - The Man Behind the Curtain
Θ 1974 - Sawyer, Juliet, Jin, Miles and Faraday join the Dharma Initiative while trying to find Locke after he disappeared into the Orchid station's Well.
Θ 1974 - Sawyer meets Richard Alpert at the Dharma Barracks and tells him that he knows about John Locke's visit to him in 1954 and about Jugghead, the nuclear bomb Daniel Faraday advised the Others to burry because it was leaking.
Θ 1977 - Jack, Hurley and Kate wake up on the Island.
5.06 - 316
Θ 1977 - Sawyer who has become chief of security for the Dharma Initiative meets up with Jin who has found Jack, Kate and Hurley by a waterfall.
Θ 1977 - During construction of The Orchid, workers discover an underground chamber that Pierre Chang warns them not to open. Time-hopping, Daniel Faraday clandestinely observes.
5.01 - Because You Left
Θ 1977 - Ben meets Richard Alpert in the jungle.
3.20 - The Man Behind The Curtain
Θ 1977 - Amy, Horace Goodspeed's new wife gives birth to a boy with Juliet's assistance.

5.17 - The Incident (2)

1980's

Θ October 27 1981 - Claire Littleton is born to Carole Littleton and Christian Shephard.
Θ 1988 - Danielle Rousseau and her crew arrive on the Island from Tahiti after hearing a transmission of the Numbers. She is later trapped at a location near the Black Rock by a "he/it" which "killed them all.". She "mercy kills" all of the members of her science crew in order to save them from the their sickness. Then, Rouseau, goes to the radio tower and replaces the Numbers broadcast (that she heard at sea during the science expedition) with a distress signal. Her daughter, Alexandra Rousseau is born and is kidnapped by the Others a week after her birth.

1990's

Θ 1991 - Sayid serves as Republican Guard and meets Kelvin Joe Inman, CIA.
Θ 1991 - Kelvin Joe Inman, CIA, serves as Interrogator/Torturer aka Intelligence Gatherer for the US military during the 1st Persian Gulf War.
Θ 1991 - Sayid crosses paths with Sam Austen (Kate's stepfather) at the end of Desert Storm in Kuwait
2.14 - One of Them
Θ October 5 1992 - John Locke's mother is admitted to the Santa Rosa Mental Health Institute to be treated for schizophrenia.
Θ December 19 1992 - The Purge takes place, ending of the conflict between the DHARMA Initiative and The Hostiles or natives of the Island, takes place. The Hostiles, with the help of Benjamin Linus, release a toxic gas from the Tempest station. This effectively wipes out the DHARMA Initiative's presence on the Island. The Purge occurs at 4 P.M. on December 19, 1992. Ben's birthday.
3.20 - The Man Behind The Curtain
Θ 1993 - Mikhail Bakunin claims to have arrived on the Island.
Θ 1995 - A week before his wedding to Ruth, Desmond gets drunk and is found in the street by Brother Campbell. Desmond joins the monastery.
Θ 1995 - Brother Campbell has a framed photo of Ms. Hawking on his desk in the monastery.
3.17 - Catch-22
Θ 1995 - Upon being expelled from the monastery, Desmond meets Penelope Widmore.
3.17 - Catch-22
Θ 1996 - Desmond asks Charles Widmore for his permission to marry his daughter Penny but is refused, meets Charlie busking on a London street, and finally breaks up with Penny. Desmond later goes back into 1996, and considers changing his history, proposing to Penny. When he goes to buy a ring, Ms. Hawking reminds him he "has second thoughts and walks right out that door" then confronts him about his destiny. Ultimately, Desmond breaks up with Penny again and throws the ring in the Thames.
3.08 - Flashes Before Your Eyes
Θ 1996 - While in basic training, Desmond begins to experience shifts between the current year and December 24, 2004, when his future self travels from the Island to the freighter Kahana. Under advice from Daniel Faraday, he goes to visit Faraday's past self at Queens College, Oxford University. Desmond goes to Penny's new house, asking for her phone number so that he can call her eight years later, on Christmas Eve, 2004. The concurrence of his meeting with Penny and his call in 2004 seem to end the shifts.
4.05 - The Constant
Θ May 1996 - Michael Dawson learns Susan Lloyd (Walt's mother) is seing another man, enraged he tells her he's coming to get his son back, crosses a street in NYC without looking, and gets hit by a car.
1.14 - Special
Θ December 24 1996 - Charles Widmore wins the journal of the first mate of the Black Rock at a Southfield's auction, after which he gives Desmond Penelope Widmore's new address.
4.05 - The Constant
Θ April 04 1999 - Desmond completes his prison sentence and is dishonorably discharged from the military. Charles Widmore waits in a car outside the prison on the day Desmond is released in order to to bribe Desmond to forget Penelope Widmore. Widmore shows Desmond that he has been intercepting Desmond's letters to Penelope Widmore for years, and informs him that Penelope Widmore is engaged to another man.
